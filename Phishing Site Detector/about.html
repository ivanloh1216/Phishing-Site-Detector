Introducting PhiSsed-Off: Your Guardian Against Phishing Attacks!

What is this extension for?

As the name suggest, PSO is a powerful Chrome extension designed to provide you with an extra layer of security while browsing the web. With the increasing prevalence of phishing attacks, it has become more important than ever to stay vigilant and protect yourself from malicious websites that aim to steal your sensitive information.

Key features:

Real-time Phishing Detection, Enhanced Site Ratings, Bookmarks, Customizable Settings, Regular Updates and Support

How to whitelist or bookmark a website I trust?

Simply click onto the nice-looking bookmark button and bookmark any of your favourite URL so that it will be flagged as safe!

What should I do if I found a new phishing site that has not been detected?

Fear not, just click on the Report Scam button to report that URL to us!
