Privacy

The extension does not track your web browsing habits or collect any personal information. You may use it without signing up for an account or logging in. The extension allows for full anonymity while use. The URL that you bookmarked is only stored in your current device, and not kept by us.

The only time that information is collected from users is when reporting a malicious URL. Only the information necessary to perform the flagging of the malicious website is collected, namely the suspicious URL.
